A Tribute to Mac Miller

American Rapper

About

Mac miller was a millenial hiphop artist who many grew up listening to. As he aged and grew as a person Mac Millers songs and artistry also evolved alongside him. A role model of strong work ethic and playful nature Mac Miller also struggled with mental issues and succame to the vice of an opiod overdose. His remaining work "Circles" captures the final moments of Mac Millers outlook on life and his struggles. THIS is Mac Miller, and THIS is his life's work.
