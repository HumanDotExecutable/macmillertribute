A Tribute to Mac Miller

American Rapper

About

Mac miller was a millennial hip-hop artist who many grew up listening to. As he aged and grew as a person, Mac Miller's songs and artistry also evolved alongside him. A role model of strong work ethic and playful nature, Mac Miller also struggled with mental issues and succumbed to the vice of an opioid overdose. His remaining work "Circles" captures the final moments of Mac Miller's outlook on life and his struggles. THIS is Mac Miller, and THIS is his life's work.
